Common topics

  • Notifications — Allow notifications for CurbTime in iOS Settings so you get reminders before your deadline.
  • Live Activities — If the countdown doesn’t appear on the Lock Screen, open Settings → CurbTime → Live Activities and ensure they’re enabled. Live Activities may not show in all situations or for very long deadlines.
  • Photos — Optional: attach a photo of the street sign or meter for the spot where you parked. Images stay on your device with your reminder.
